Why Ganesh puja celebrate

Impressed by his love for the parents and intelligence in winning the race, Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati blessed Lord Ganesh with the fruit of knowledge along with that of immortality. This is the reason why we seek blessing from Lord Ganesha before beginning anything new.



When We Celebrate Ganesh Puja

Ganesh Chaturthi is also celebrated as the birth anniversary of Lord Ganesha, which is celebrated as per the Hindu calendar in the Bhadrapada month. The shubh muhurat will begin from 12.17 am to 10 pm on 31 August. In fact, the best time to begin the puja will start from 11.03 am to 01.33 pm on 31 Augest.


Where We Celebrate Ganesh Puja

This festival is celebrated with great devotion and joy by Hindus throughout the world. In India, it is majorly celebrated in states including Maharashtra, Gujarat, Goa, Madhya Pradesh, Karnataka, and Telangana.
